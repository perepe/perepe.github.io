Duel

Duel is a strategy game set in the far west where you are a brave cowboy with one single goal: kill your opponent before he kills you!

This is how this western game works, you have three options to choose from:

  • Shoot: If you have ammo and your rival doesn't use his shield, you will score 1 point.
  • Reload: If you run out of bullets, you will need to reload your ammo.
  • Shield: You can protect yourself using a pan as a shield.
Downloads: Android iOS (sometime in the future)

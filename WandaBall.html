Wanda Ball

Wanda ball is an arcade 2D basketball game where you can control the trajectory of the ball by transforming it into another kind of ball (bowling, etc) when it is in the air. The different balls and powers (slow motion, etc) are unlocked as the player progresses through the game.

There are 24 levels to unlock and 4 modes:

  • Simple shot: get the shot as fast as possible.
  • Star shot: try to get as many stars as you can before scoring.
  • Countdown shot: get as many shots as you can before the countdown ends.
  • Free shot: Play as much as you want to master the level.
Downloads: PC Android iOS (sometime in the future)
